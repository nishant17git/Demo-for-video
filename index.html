At AGCT, we believe in the spirit of competitive cricket — a game of strategy, endurance, and unwavering passion. The AGCT Men’s Cricket Test Championship brings together aspiring cricketers in a classic Round Robin format, with the top two teams battling it out in the grand finale. With registrations yet to open, the excitement builds for this stage of raw talent, skill, and the purest form of cricket.
